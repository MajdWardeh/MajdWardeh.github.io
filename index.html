Master's Thesis

Vision-based Autonomous Drone Racing

End-to-end system for autonomous quadrotor navigation in racing scenarios, relying solely on onboard sensing and compute. The pipeline combines:

  • Perception Network: detects racing gates and regresses 2D corner coordinates.
  • Policy Network: generates 4D Bézier-parameterized trajectory segments using perception outputs and vehicle state.

Achieved ~90% gate traversal success at speeds up to 5 m/s, demonstrating robust closed-loop performance suitable for ADR competitions.
